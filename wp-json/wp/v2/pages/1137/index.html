One thing that should be noted is that at Objektiv Digital, we are always seeking to innovate. In an attempt to innovate, we put Prime’s website on a newer platform that unfortunately created too many issues both for Jennifer and us as a company. For no additional charge, we rebuilt Jennifer’s website on a more conventional platform (WordPress) and made a seamless transition to technology that will serve Prime better.<\/p>\t\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t<\/section>\n\t\t\t\t
