The
 Challenge

John Page at Islander Plumbing and Heating knew that to get a leg up on his competition, a professionally designed and developed website can give him an edge over other plumbers in the Comox Valley. He wanted his website to inform customers about what he offers, and more importantly, to bring more leads and sales in his email inbox and ringing his phone number.

The
Objectives

Generate leads & sales

Inform visitors about services

Contact & quote forms

A design that stands out

The
Solution

Working closely with John, we devised a landing page formulated to convert visitors into leads and customers by making it compelling and easy to click the Call to Action button – to request a quote. We touched on his customers’ problems, presented John as a guide to providing their solution, and presented him as the authority that is empathetic to their situation.

The
Results

When John contacted us in June, he needed a website. One month letter, guess what John had? A website! Not only that, a website that looks great (and we dare say, better than his competition). A website that reflects the superior quality and beyond the call of duty service that John and his team provides. We had a great time making his website, and are grateful to be his digital partner.
